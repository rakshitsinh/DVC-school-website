About Us

We are a school with an Indian mind, an Indian heart and an Indian soul. We wish to inject the minds of our students with quest for excellence and with strength of character. We are prepared to provide the holistic development of body, mind and spirit. We have a firm belief that an overall development of personality will help a child to face the world with ease, enthusiasm and vigour. We believe that apart from academic betterment, one needs to learn soft skills, like personality development, culture and should imbibe ethical values.
